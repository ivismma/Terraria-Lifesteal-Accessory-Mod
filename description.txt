Adds an hardmode accessory item in the Crimson and Corruption biomes:

Vampire Claw:

- Life Steal: 4%
- Life Steal effect does not apply in PvP
- Grants bonus 4% attack speed to melee weapons.

- Near Death "Passive" : When below 15% health, receives an additional small boost of lifesteal and attack speed, going up to 6% Lifesteal and 8% melee attack speed.

How to get: Mob drop

- Drop Chance:
0.5% - Herpling (Crimson)
0.5% - Corruptor (Corruption)
